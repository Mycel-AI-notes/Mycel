## How to sign

We use two equivalent mechanisms; either is sufficient.

### Option A — CLA Assistant bot (preferred)

When you open your first pull request, the [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io/) bot will post a comment asking you to sign. Click the link, sign in with GitHub, and accept. Your signature is recorded against your GitHub account and applies to all future contributions to this repository.

### Option B — Manual statement

If the CLA Assistant is unavailable, leave a comment on your pull request containing exactly:

> I have read the Mycel CLA v1.0 and I agree to it. — *(Your Full Name, GitHub @handle)*

A maintainer will record your acceptance. This statement covers all your future Contributions to the Project unless you withdraw it in writing.

## Withdrawing acceptance

You may withdraw consent for **future** Contributions at any time by emailing the maintainers or opening an issue titled `CLA withdrawal: @your-handle`. Withdrawal does not affect Contributions already submitted and accepted under this Agreement.
